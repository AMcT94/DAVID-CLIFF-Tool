The CLIFF Dashboard details the long-term financial outlook for different career paths. The Dashboard shows income and public assistance receipt over a 20-year-plus horizon. The Dashboard also includes a household budget target that identifies the estimated level at which a worker’s after-tax income covers the cost of basic househould budget items necessary to live and work in the modern economy.

Step 1: Complete ALL fields to the left.


Step 2: Click the 'Calculate Results' button. A progress indicator will display as the tool calculates the results.


Step 3: CLIFF will then automatically direct to the 'Results' tab and display how much a typical individual in the selected career path may expect to earn over time, how those earnings compare to the household budget target, how those earnings may impact public assistance, and other results.


Step 4: To make changes to the selections, adjust the dropdown menus to the left to and click 'Recalculate' to update the results.
